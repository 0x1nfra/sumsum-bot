| paper execution -> SQLite ledger | Entry, lifecycle, and bankroll events cross into durable local storage |

| T-04-02 | T | `core/storage.py` | mitigate | Persist paper lifecycle and bankroll history explicitly so settlement cannot silently overwrite entry state |
