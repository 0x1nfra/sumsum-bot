<threat_model>
## Trust Boundaries

| Boundary | Description |
|----------|-------------|
| risk decision -> paper execution | Only persisted `ALLOWED` decisions may cross into position creation. |
| Polymarket resolution payload -> settlement logic | External market outcome data is untrusted and may be incomplete or ambiguous. |
| execution service -> SQLite ledger | Lifecycle history must not be silently overwritten or collapsed into mutable latest-only state. |

## STRIDE Threat Register

| Threat ID | Category | Component | Disposition | Mitigation Plan |
|-----------|----------|-----------|-------------|-----------------|
| T-04-01-01 | T | `core/paper_execution.py` settlement resolver | mitigate | Validate outcome branches explicitly and fail closed with `settlement_blocked` plus resolver reason codes when the market is closed but outcome data is missing or conflicting. |
| T-04-01-02 | R | `core/storage.py` paper ledger tables | mitigate | Use append-only `paper_position_events` and `bankroll_snapshots` rows so entry and settlement history remains auditable instead of mutable-only. |
| T-04-01-03 | D | `core/paper_execution.py` duplicate settlement path | mitigate | Make settlement idempotent against already-resolved positions and only emit one terminal lifecycle event per `paper_position_id`. |
</threat_model>
